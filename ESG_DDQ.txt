Appendix E        ESG DD Questionnaire
The Fund defines thresholds for the ESG DD questionnaire. Areas where an investee does not meet the Fund’s threshold require an Action Plan item. Levels below the threshold are coloured yellow, while levels above the threshold are coloured green.

AML, KYC & KYB
Management
	

Non-existent
	

	Level 0
	

	The company engages in ad-hoc Know Your Customer (KYC), Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Business (KYB) checks. Incidents are reported to GGV.
	Level 1
	The company conducts regular AML, KYC & KYB checks. Staff are trained to report suspected money laundering. AML awareness training is provided for select staff. Incidents are reported to GGV.
	Level 2
	The company conducts regular AML, KYC & KYB checks. A designated AML officer reports results to the Board. Staff and business partners are informed about the AML, KYC & KYB practices and screened for previous convictions. AML training is mandatory for all staff and incidents are reported to GGV.
